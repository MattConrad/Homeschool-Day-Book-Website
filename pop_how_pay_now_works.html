When you click on the "Pay Now" button, you will be taken to a new webpage hosted by PayPal. You will not be on the Homeschool Day Book web site any more.

You can use this page to pay with either a regular credit card or a PayPal account.

On this page, you will see the product you are buying (Homeschool Day Book), and a place to enter your billing information (your name, credit card info, address, and contact information).

(If you want to use a PayPal account to pay, you can log in instead of entering a credit card. Ignore the PayPal log in if you want to use a regular credit card.)

Once you've entered your billing information, click the "Review Order and Continue" button. This will take you to a new page where you can review the details of your purchase and your contact information. Make sure everything is correct, then click "Pay Now". Your credit card will be charged, we'll be notified of your payment, and we'll send your license key to unlock Homeschool Day Book for permanent use.

Note that we never see your financial details. When you enter your credit card, you are doing it on PayPal's secure website. The information you enter there is encoded to keep it safe during transmission. PayPal handles the actual transaction--we just get the results. Your credit card number stays in the hands of the credit card experts.

If you have other questions or concerns about the payment process, please contact us at info@homeschooldaybook.com.
